Interesting Facts about Cicada

  • Cicadas avoid the predators by singing at night.
  • Cicadas have the unique ability to turn themselves into evaporative coolers during extremely hot weather.
  • Cicadas have beautiful transparent wings which if you hold against a light source will transmit rainbow hues.

Interesting Facts about Panda

  • Panda likes to live alone.
  • Being carnivores, pandas can exhibit extreme bite power more than any other carnivore.
  • Though carnivorous, pandas primarily like to feed on bamboo sprouts and leaves.
